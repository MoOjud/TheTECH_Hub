About Us

Founded in 2022, Tech Hub connects the world's professionals to make them more productive and successful.
We aim to create economic opportunity for every member of the global workforce.

Tech Hub serves to create a peer-to-peer marketplace that serves both IT professionals and clients with projects that need the services of these professionals.
It would be a platform where IT professionals can itemize their relevant skills and experiences to provide freelance services to individuals or companies that need them.
